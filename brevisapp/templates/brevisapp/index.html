...who we are

Brevis was started by an editor with a dream. She dreamed of menus with no typos, perfectly spelled Tweets, and signs without randomly inserted apostrophes. With that, Brevis was born.

what we do...

Our goal is to provide fast, accessible, reliable, and error-free editing at such a low cost you can't really afford not to use us. Our top-notch editors have been carefully vetted to ensure immaculate results on every project—guaranteed.

...how it works

We want to provide our clients easy access to quality editing. Every project is priced per word, and you can choose whether you want your project back in four hours (4¢ per word) or fourty-eight hours (2¢ per word).
